## Railcar Event Definitions


### To Yard
* **arrived**: car stops at yard
* **uncoupled**: car is physically separated from its existing train consist
* **set out**: car is placed in yard storage

### Yard Operations
* **classified**: car is assigned to an outbound train consist
* **inspection started**: car inspection begins
* **inspection finished**: car inspection ends
* **bad order**: car is determined to be mechanically defective
* **repair started**: car repair begins
* **repair finished**: car repair ends

### From Yard
* **picked up**: car is retrieved from yard storage
* **coupled**: car is physically connected to a train consist 
* **departed**: car leaves yard

### To Industry
* **arrived**: car stops at industry location
* **uncoupled**: car is physically separated from its existing train consist
* **spotted**: car is positioned for industry operations

### Industry Operations
* **load started**: car loading beings
* **load finished**: car loading ends
* **unload started**: car unloading begins
* **unload finished**: car unloading ends
* **released**: car is determined to be picked up from industry location

### From Industry
* **picked up**: car is retrieved from industry location
* **coupled**: car is physically connected to a train consist 
* **departed**: car leaves industry location
