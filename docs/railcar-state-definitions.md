## Railcar State Definitions


### Servicing
* **Acquired** (A): car is added to service
* **Retired** (R): car is removed from service

### Yard
* **To Yard** (TY): car is in transit to yard
* **Switching** (SW): car is being moved around yard
* **At Yard** (AY): car is sitting idle at yard

### Yard Storage
* **To Storage** (TYS): car is being moved to yard storage
* **In Storage** (IYS): car is sitting idle in yard storage
* **From Storage** (FYS): car is being moved from yard storage

### Yard Operations
* **Under Inspection** (UI): car is being inspected
* **Bad Order** (BA): car is determined to be mechanically defective
* **Under Repair** (UR): car is being repaired

### Industry
* **To Industry** (TI): car is in transit to industry
* **At Industry** (AI): car is sitting idle at industry 

### Industry Spur
* **To Spur** (TIS): car is being moved to industry spur
* **At Spur** (AIS): car is sitting idle at industry spur
* **From Spur** (FIS): car is being moved from industry spur

### Industry Operations
* **Loading** (L): car is being loaded with freight at industry spur
* **Unloading** (U): car freight is being unloaded at industry spur
